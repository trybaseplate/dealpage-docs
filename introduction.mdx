## What is DealPage?
DealPage is a software platform designed for technical pre-sales teams, enabled by an AI named Paige. Paige can help you answer questions ('RFIs') from teammates, stakeholders, partners, and prospects more efficiently.
This frees you up to do what you do best - focus on your customers.

## Who is DealPage for?
| Role | Use Case                           |
| -------- | ------------------------------------- |
| SEs / Solutions | <ul><li>Provide technical support to reps and AEs </li><li>Prep for demos by reviewing discovery and finding best playbooks </li><li>Respond to emails, RFPs, and Security Questionnaires </li></ul> |
| AEs / BDRs  | <ul><li>Get answers to questions about products instantly</li><li>Generate proposals and other sales content </li><li>Write personalized follow-up emails and share content </li></ul>                        |
| Managers   | <ul><li>Get caught up on deal activity </li><li>Understand best practices and build playbooks</li><li>Spend less time hunting for information </li></ul> |

## What is DealPage?
DealPage connects your most important GTM data sources to an AI assistant. This allows you to turn your existing data into novel insights, sales collateral, and answers to your most pressing questions.

Connect your CRM, email, meeting transcripts, and other documentation tools in a few clicks to get started.

Paige (your AI assistant) is trained to navigate these tools and resources for you. She uses agentic behavior to select resources based on her task, and then advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to generate factual responses.

## Why use DealPage?
1. **Insights**: Get unique insights into your team's performance, your pipeline, and customer engagement.
2. **Efficiency**: Automate workflows and generate content in seconds, saving 8+ hours of work per deal.
3. **Accuracy**: DealPage automatically uses the latest, most accurate version of your tribal knowledge, reducing human errors by up to 95%.

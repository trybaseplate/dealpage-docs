## What is DealPage?
DealPage is a software platform designed for pre-sales teams. Our AI Sales Engineer, Paige, helps presales teams manage their knowledge, automate workflows, and generate unique insights.

## Why use DealPage?
1. **Speed**: Our AI-powered search engine makes it 90% faster to find answers compared to traditional content libraries.
2. **Accuracy**: DealPage automatically uses the latest, most accurate version of your tribal knowledge, reducing human errors by up to 95%.
3. **Efficiency**: Automate workflows and generate content in seconds, saving 8+ hours of work per week.
4. **Insights**: Get unique insights into your team's performance, your pipeline, and customer engagement.
